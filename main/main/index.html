WELCOME TO OUR DEPARTMENT

DISCOVER EXCELLENCE IN ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

Vision

Facilitating the development of competent professionals in Electrical and Electronics Engineering capable of keeping pace with changing technologies and providing service to society.

Mission

- Imparting strong fundamental and applied knowledge in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
- Developing professionalism through innovative practices and industry engagement.
- Enhancing entrepreneurship skills in emerging technologies of the energy sector in-line with national requirements.
- Providing sustainable solutions to the challenges faced by rural society.

We take pride in our dedication to excellence and innovation, shaping the future of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. With expertise in advancing technologies, research, and education, we create impactful solutions for a sustainable and connected world.

CoE E-Mobility

The Centre of Excellence in E-Mobility, in collaboration with Decibels Lab, drives innovation in electric vehicle technologies, offering advanced research, hands-on training, and industry-focused solutions to accelerate sustainable mobility advancements.

Coe Industrial Automation

The Centre of Excellence in Industrial Automation, established in collaboration with Festo, focuses on advancing automation technologies through specialized training, cutting-edge research, and industry-aligned solutions, empowering future-ready professionals and sustainable industrial growth.

IoT Research Centre

The IoT Research Centre, focused on advancing Internet of Things and Smart Grid technologies, fosters innovation through cutting-edge research, hands-on experimentation, and collaboration with industry partners, driving smart, sustainable solutions for a connected future.
